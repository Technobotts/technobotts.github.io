Welcome to the website of the TechnoBotts robotics team, which currently consists of two boys aged 16 and 17 from villages near Cambridge.

TechnoBotts are the 2011 UK RoboCup Junior Soccer champions!
The Robocup Soccer UK Final 2011 Demonstrating the robots at the Cavendish laboratory in Cambridge The trophy which we won at the UK competition

We have been the UK RoboCup Junior Soccer champions for two years running, and went to Singapore to represent the UK in RoboCup in 2010. Previously, we travelled to Atlanta in 2007 and 2009 to compete in First Lego League (FLL).

This year, after doing even better in the 2011 UK final (we scored 28 goals in the 2011 UK final and let no goals in), we will be travelling to Istanbul to represent the UK in RoboCup 2011. You can find information this year's robots here.

Previously, we have travelled to Atlanta in 2007 and 2009 to compete in First Lego League (FLL). In the 2009 competition we won the first place trophy for Innovative Design.
